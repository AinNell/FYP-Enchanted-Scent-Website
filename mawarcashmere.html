Mawar Cashmere

| 50ML |

Description

Mawar Cashmere, an unisex fragrance that encapsulates the essence of sophistication and opulence. Meticulously crafted with an exceptional blend of captivating notes, Mawar Cashmere is an olfactory journey that will transport you to a realm of enchantment.

At the heart of Mawar Cashmere lies the bewitching allure of roses, their delicate petals exuding an intoxicating floral aroma. This iconic flower, symbolizing love and beauty, forms the soul of this fragrance, infusing it with timeless elegance and unparalleled grace. Embark on this fragrant adventure, prepare to be captivated by the irresistible charm of the rose, unveiling its multifaceted facets with every delicate whiff.

Mawar Cashmere, where the delicacy of roses meets the opulence of cashmere, where sensuality intertwines with elegance. Experience the magic of this extraordinary fragrance, as it gracefully lingers on the skin, leaving an indelible mark of sophistication and allure.

Notes

Rose, Cashmeran, Musk, Saffron, Amber, Vanilla, Clove, Cedarwood, Raspberry.
