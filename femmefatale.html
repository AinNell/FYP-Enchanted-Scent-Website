Femme Fatale

| 30ML |

Description

Awaken your inner enchantress with this alluring fragrance. Delicate tuberose and creamy almond intertwine with hints of nutty coconut and vanilla, reminiscent of Dior Hypnotic Poison & Mmm Juliette Has A Gun.

It's a captivating blend that exudes confidence and allure, ideal for those mesmerizing moments when you want to leave an unforgettable impression.
